  • Theology. For All.

    Publishing classic theological texts in contemporary digital formats.

    This company began when the founders saw the pressing need for Syntax of the Moods and Tenses to be published in an electronic format. For the beginning Greek student, this perennial grammar on Greek Verbs requires constant referencing and in-depth study and reflection in order to grasp the nuances of Greek verbs. Frustrated with having to constantly thumb back and forth through Burton's grammar rules, we set out to create an hyperlinked and interactive version of Syntax. After three months of squashing typos and wrangling HTML, we finished making the electronic version. When we first used Syntax, we saw how immensely effective Burton was in this format. We could touch, click, read and navigate around Syntax with ease. This meant that we could spend more time reading and understanding, and less time flipping pages and searching for grammar rules. It then dawned upon us that Christians could be greatly served if electronic editions of classic Christian texts were produced and published to a worldwide audience. Today, Fig publishes theological content in every open-standard electronic format. In addition, we create learning software for biblical studies and theology. It is our conviction that every Christian should be intellectually challenged and learned in their faith. Soli Deo Gloria!
